Krists Ziders is a young driver, who started his karting career at the age of 10. By going to summer camps in 333 autosport track, he fell in love with speed, racing and karting community. Later on he was invited to become one of "333 Autosport" team members and began his career in Rotax MiniMax Class. With time he became more skilled and quicly moved up to Junior Class, competed with best f Baltic and Russian drivers until he was ready and grown up enough to join one of the fastest and most dangerous of classes - Rotax Max DD2. This opportunity made Krists enter the Europian level races and championships in tracks like Le Mans (France), Genk: Home Of Champions (Belgium), Campillos (Italy) and other significant tracks in Spain, Portugal, Hungary etc.
